The 2011 RuleML Symposium

The International Symposium on Rules, RuleML, has evolved from an annual series of international workshops since 2002, international conferences in 2005 and 2006, and international symposia since 2007. In 2011 two instalments of the RuleML Symposium will take place.

RuleML 2011 Europe

RuleML2011-Europe is the first of the two 2011 RuleML events. It will be held in conjunction with IJCAI 2011 (International Joint Conference on Artificial Intelligence) in Barcelona, Spain, in July.

A selection of the best papers will be presented during a joint session with IJCAI, and the authors of such papers will be invited to submit a revised version of their papers for inclusion in the IJCAI proceedings.

RuleML 2011 America

The second instalment, RuleML2011-America, will be co-located with the Business Rules Forum 2011 to be held in early November in Fort Lauderdale, Florida, USA.

RuleML-2011 America will include the RuleML Challenge Award that this year will be dedicate to Rules and Ontologies.
