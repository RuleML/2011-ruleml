In 2011 two instalments of the RuleML Symposium will take place. The first one, <a href="http://2011.ruleml.org/europe">RuleML2011-Europe</a>, will be held in conjunction with <a href="http://ijcai-11.iiia.csic.es/">IJCAI 2011</a> (International Joint Conference on Artificial Intelligence) in Barcelona, Spain, in July. The second, <a href="http://2011.ruleml.org/america">RuleML2011-America</a>, will be co-located with the <a href="http://www.businessrulesforum.com/" title="BUSINESS RULES FORUM || October 30 - November 3, 2011 || Fort Lauderdale, FL || Achieving True Business Agliity">Business Rules Forum 2011</a> to be held in early November in Fort Lauderdale, Florida, USA.
