Visa

We would like to remind RuleML 2011 participants of the visa requirements for travellers to the European Union. International registrants should be particularly aware and careful about visa requirements, and should plan travel well in advance. If you need a visa, please send your request for a letter in support of a visa application to ruleml2011ijcai AT easychair.org. Include your name, physical mailing address, fax number. Authors of papers should include the title. Visa support letters cannot be issued via email.
