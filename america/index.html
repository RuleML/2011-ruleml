The 5th International RuleML Symposium on Rules will be held from 3rd-5th Nov 2011 at the Westin Diplomat Resort, Fort Lauderdale, Florida, USA.

RuleML2011@BRF is the second part of RuleML 2011 co-located with  International Business Rules Forum, the premier industry conference on business rules.
The first part is RuleML2011@IJCAI, to be held in Barcelona, Spain from 19-21 July 2011 and co-located with the 22nd International Joint Conference on Artificial Intelligence (IJCAI).
